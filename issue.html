Library Management System

Library Card:

Obtain a library card if you don't already have one. This usually involves filling out an application form and providing proof of identification and address.

Browse the Collection:

Explore the library's collection to find the book you want. You can use the library's catalog, either online or in print, to locate the book's call number and its availability.

Retrieve the Book:

Once you've identified the book you want, locate it on the library shelves using the call number. If you have trouble finding it, library staff or volunteers are usually available to assist.

Check Availability:

Confirm the availability of the book. If the book is currently checked out by another patron, you may need to place a hold or request it to be held for you once it is returned.
